General Questions

The Sheikh Isaqow Hassan Bora app is an Islamic education platform that provides access to authentic lectures, teachings, and religious content from Sheikh Isaqow Hassan Bora. The app aims to make Islamic knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of their location.

Yes, the app is completely free to download and use. All content, including lectures and educational materials, is available at no cost. Our mission is to spread Islamic knowledge freely to everyone.

Sheikh Isaqow Hassan Bora is an Islamic scholar with extensive education in Islamic studies. He holds a Bachelor's degree from Imam Shafi'i University in Adama, Ethiopia, and a Master's degree from the Islamic University of Minnesota, Ethiopia Branch. Learn more on our About page.

The app primarily features content in Arabic and Somali, reflecting the languages used by Sheikh Isaqow Hassan Bora in his teachings. The app interface is available in English for broader accessibility.

Features & Usage

Yes! The app includes offline functionality. You can download lectures and content to your device and access them without an internet connection. This is perfect for learning on the go or in areas with limited connectivity.

The app features an intuitive interface with easy navigation. From the home screen, you can browse different categories of lectures, search for specific topics, access your downloaded content, and manage your favorites. Simply tap on any lecture to start listening.

We regularly update the app with new lectures and content from Sheikh Isaqow Hassan Bora. Updates are typically released monthly, and you'll receive notifications when new content is available. Make sure to keep your app updated to access the latest teachings.

Yes, you can share lectures and content with friends and family. Each lecture has a share button that allows you to send links via messaging apps, social media, or email. Spreading beneficial knowledge is encouraged!

Technical Support

The app requires Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher. This ensures compatibility with most modern Android devices while maintaining optimal performance and security.

The app itself is lightweight, requiring approximately 20-30 MB. However, if you download lectures for offline listening, you'll need additional storage space depending on how much content you download. We recommend having at least 100 MB of free space available.

Try these troubleshooting steps: 1) Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed. 2) Clear the app cache in your device settings. 3) Restart your device. 4) If the problem persists, please contact us through the Contact page with details about the issue.

Yes, we take your privacy and security seriously. The app collects minimal data necessary for functionality, and we do not share your personal information with third parties. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

We are currently focused on providing the best experience for Android users. An iOS version is being considered for future development based on user demand and resources. Stay tuned for updates!
