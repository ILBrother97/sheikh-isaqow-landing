About Sheikh Isaqow Hassan Bora

الشيخ اسحاقو حسن بورا آدم المويالي من مواليد عام 1980 ميلادي. درس المرحلتين الإبتدائية والمتوسطة في مركز الهدى الإسلامي بمويالي كينيا، والمرحلة الثانوية في معهد نور الإسلام بمنديرا كينيا، بالإضافة إلى معهد اللغة العربية ودروس الحلقات في مويالي ومنديرا في المساجد.

تخرج في جامعة الإمام الشافعي في مدينة أداما إثيوبيا بمرحلة البكالوريوس، ثم تخرج من مرحلة الماجستير في الجامعة الإسلامية بمنيسوتا فرع إثيوبيا.

About the Developer

Building technology for Islamic education

This app was developed with a clear mission: to make authentic Islamic education accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or circumstances. As a developer passionate about both technology and Islamic knowledge, I saw an opportunity to bridge the gap between traditional Islamic scholarship and modern digital platforms.

Sheikh Isaqow Hassan Bora's teachings have benefited countless individuals, and this app serves as a digital extension of his educational mission. By leveraging modern Android development technologies, we've created a platform that allows users to access valuable Islamic content anytime, anywhere.

The app is built with user experience in mind, featuring offline access, organized content, and a clean interface that makes learning enjoyable. We continue to improve and add new features based on user feedback, ensuring that the app remains a valuable resource for the Muslim community.
