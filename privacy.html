Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your personal information

Last updated: November 12, 2024

Introduction

At Sheikh Isaqow Hassan Bora, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Islamic education app and website.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (type, operating system, version)
  • App usage statistics (features used, time spent)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, language preferences)
  • Analytics data to improve app performance

Information You Provide

  • Contact information when you reach out to us
  • Feedback and suggestions you submit
  • App preferences and settings

How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide and maintain the app's features
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • Support: To respond to your questions and provide technical support
  • Communication: To send important updates about the app
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our content

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the app (analytics, hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Safety: To prevent fraud or protect user safety

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure servers, and regular security assessments.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics or certain data collection features

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Analytics: For usage analytics and insights
  • App Distribution Platforms: APKPure, APK.COM for app downloads
  • Hosting Services: Netlify for website hosting

International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
